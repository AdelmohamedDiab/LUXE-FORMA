About LUXE FORMA

Crafting Spaces,
Defining Luxury

Founded in 2008, LUXE FORMA was born from a singular vision: to create furniture that transcends the ordinary. Every piece in our collection is a product of rigorous design, master craftsmanship, and the world's finest materials.

From our ateliers in Milan and Copenhagen, we source sustainably and build with purpose — ensuring that each piece you bring home carries not just beauty, but a story.

  • Sustainably sourced premium materials
  • Handcrafted by master artisans
  • 10-year structural warranty
  • Custom bespoke commissions available
